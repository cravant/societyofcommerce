About the organization

A national platform for commerce, leadership, and opportunity.

Society of Commerce was founded in 2026 to help communities build stronger professional networks and give students a practical pathway into entrepreneurship.

Mission

Connect leaders who create value.

Our mission is to bring together entrepreneurs, professionals, students, business owners, and community leaders through high-quality events, education, mentorship, and chapter-based leadership.

History

Founded for the next generation of builders.

Founded in 2026, Society of Commerce began with a simple premise: serious business relationships should be easier to build, and leadership opportunities should start earlier. The organization is designed to grow through city chapters, high school chapters, regional leadership, and a national operating structure.
