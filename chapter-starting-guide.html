Chapter starting guide

Bring Society of Commerce to your city or school.

A step-by-step guide for launching a professional, credible, and nationally aligned chapter.

Requirements

What a chapter needs to begin.

Each chapter should begin with committed organizers, a clear audience, a meeting plan, and alignment with Society of Commerce standards.

City chapter

Founding organizer, leadership team, venue plan, member recruitment list, and event calendar.

High school chapter

Student officers, faculty advisor, school approval, meeting plan, and service or business project.

Step 1

Submit interest form

Share your city, school, leadership background, and chapter type.

Step 2

Recruit founding leaders

Identify officers, advisors, committee chairs, and initial members.

Step 3

Complete application

Submit the chapter charter, proposed calendar, and operating plan.

Step 4

Approval and onboarding

National leadership reviews, approves, and provides launch resources.

Step 5

Launch first event

Host a kickoff meeting, networking night, or student business session.

Application process

Clear stages from interest to launch.

The approval process confirms that each chapter is ready to represent the organization professionally and serve members consistently.

Officer positions

Recommended chapter leadership.

President

Vice President

Supports operations, programming, and committee execution.

Secretary

Maintains records, agendas, member lists, and communications.

Treasurer

Tracks budgets, sponsorship, expenses, and financial policies.
